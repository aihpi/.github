# AI Service Centre Berlin Brandenburg @ HPI

Welcome to the AI Service Centre of the Hasso Plattner Institute!
The AI Service Centre Berlin Brandenburg is one of four service centres for artificial intelligence (AI) funded by the German [Federal Ministry of Research, Technology and Space (BMFTR)](https://www.bmftr.bund.de/EN) under the funding code 16IS22092. We improve access to AI technology and lower AI's financial and technological barriers in business and society.

Find out more or contact us on our [website](https://hpi.de/kisz)!
